Pet Care Resources

Responsible ownership starts with knowledge. Here are some essential tips for new owners.

Bringing Your New Pet Home

Posted: Oct 12, 2025

The first few days in a new home are critical for a rescue animal. It is a time of transition that can be both exciting and stressful. To ensure a smooth process, create a designated "safe space" where your pet can retreat if they feel overwhelmed.

The Rule of 3-3-3

  • 3 Days: To decompress. They may sleep a lot or hide.
  • 3 Weeks: To learn routine. Their personality starts to show.
  • 3 Months: To feel at home. Trust and bond are built.

Nutrition Basics: Dogs vs. Cats

Posted: Sept 28, 2025

Understanding the dietary needs of your pet is fundamental to their longevity. While dogs are omnivores and can handle a variety of foods, cats are obligate carnivores.

Cats need Taurine: This amino acid is essential for heart health and vision in cats and is found in animal-based proteins. Never feed a cat strict dog food.

Avoid Toxic Foods: Chocolate, grapes, onions, and xylitol (found in gum) are toxic to most household pets. Keep these secured away from curious paws.

Quick Tips

Fresh water should always be available.

Exercise reduces behavioral issues.

Microchipping ensures lost pets return home.
