About Paws & Hearts

At Paws & Hearts, we are dedicated to bridging the gap between homeless pets and loving families. We provide a safe haven for animals in need, offering medical care, rehabilitation, and socialization until they find their permanent homes. Our mission is to ensure every pet is treated with dignity and every owner feels supported.

Whether you are looking to adopt a playful puppy, a calm senior cat, or simply want to learn how to better care for your current pet, we are your trusted community resource.
